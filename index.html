Hey! My name is Rejwan and i am a part of the Experimentation team of Expedia. I'm looking forward to be an apprentice at Expedia Group particularly the experimentation team because I am fascinated at how experimentation works at Expedia Group.

In the Experimentation team we are responsible for creating new features that our partners want in order to increase revenue, we implement them and then we test whether that feature would attract more bookings or not. If so then we implement the change so that all the users experiences it and if not we discard it. Our Experiements tend to last two weeks or more and may be based on different metrics such as creating a new feature that would make it easier for customers to check dates on whether what hotel is available for their holiday, this is important because if a customer puts in their dates for their holiday they are 6 times more likely to make a booking with us!
